Origin Story

"We need to spread the love."

It started with a prank. Benjamin gifted Avi 100 pounds of russet potatoes. Avi retaliated with a 5-gallon bucket of Kikkoman full sodium soy sauce. Neither of them knew what to do with it — so they founded a club.

That bucket holds 1,200 servings. Enough soy sauce for the average four-person Japanese family for an entire year. It felt wrong to keep it to themselves.
