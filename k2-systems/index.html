Internship Description

During my internship at K2 Systems US, I was involved in the development, testing, and certification of solar mounting solutions designed for the US market. The company specializes in creating innovative mounting systems that are both cost-effective and comply with strict building regulations.

A significant portion of my role involved designing and constructing tests and test jigs that could accurately simulate the stresses these mounting systems would encounter in real-world installations. These tests needed to account for various weather conditions, including high winds, snow loads, and seismic activity.

Another significant portion of my time spent in this role was dedicated to creating customer facing documentation. Varying from installation assemblies to part drawings. These documents were created for K2 Systems' customers to find on the company website.

By the end of my internship, I had successfully developed a standardized testing methodology that reduced certification time by 30% while maintaining the rigorous safety standards required in the industry as well revolutionizing the companies customer facing documents.
