FAQ

Common questions

How long are sessions?

Most sessions run 60–90 minutes. We can do shorter check-ins or longer deep-dives depending on what you need.

How far do you travel for in-person?

I cover most of San Diego County — La Jolla, Mission Valley, Chula Vista, El Cajon, and surrounding areas. Just ask if you're unsure.

How do I pay?

Venmo, Zelle, or cash — whatever's easiest. Payment after each session.

Can I get a free intro call first?

Yes, always. We'll spend 15 minutes on the phone before booking anything. No pressure, no commitment.

Do you work with adults?

Absolutely. Whether you're brushing up for a career change, tackling a continuing-ed course, or just curious — I work with adults as much as with students.

How much notice do you need?

A day or two is usually fine. Reach out and we'll find something that works.
